Choose Membership

Unique to non-profits, expand your organizations reach by joining the Lexington Chamber of Commerce to gain access to many of the valley's other top community focused businesses.

Bronze membership includes receive one Chamber window decal, opportunity to attend networking events, customizable online business listing, use the Chamber Shareholder logo in advertising, recognition in weekly e-newsletter and monthly breakfast brief, receive discounts through Shareholder Savings and Chamber Affiliate Programs.

Silver membership includes bronze benefits plus Business listing in two categories in Chamber online directory, One complimentary ribbon cutting ceremony at the Chamber or business location, and Recognition in the Chamber's Preferred Caterer List.

Gold membership includes silver benefits plus Business listing in three categories in Chamber online directory, One high-resolution headshot from JH Photography, and Business logo displays in online directory search results.
