Target Audience:

The target audience will be campers. Those who love to camp and explore trails. People who want to go on an adventure.

Scenario 1:

Some college students want to go on a hike but don't know which hike to go on. This website tells you how many trails there are, how long they are, and which one is the most popular.

Scenario 2:

A couple of old friends get together to go on a camp but they do not know where they want to go or where they can go. This website is built for the purpose of that. They can check out this website and see where they can go.

Scenario 3:

This person is wanting to go on an adventure but doesn't know what to bring with him or even know if he should go with someone on this hike. This website is built for that as well. Telling you what to bring and if you should go with someelse on this hike.

Scenario 4:

A family wants to go on a camp but don't know how camp. They also don't know how to setup a camp. This website is built for that as well. It will give the guidence and knowledge on how to setup a camp and how to go on a camp.

Scenario 5:

There are a couple of friends who want to go on a camp and know how to setup a camp but don't know how to setup a fire and the safety items they should bring to a camp. This website is also built for that as well. Telling you how to setup a fire and the steps you should take before starting up a fire. It also tells you what safety items you can bring with you to a camp.
